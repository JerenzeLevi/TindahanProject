Our Story

COMMUNITY FIRST

This Tindahan is a simple business built with one goal: convenience. We wanted to provide a reliable mini-shop right here in our community, helping our neighbors save time and effort.

Instead of traveling far away to the city for daily essentials, you can find what you need just a few walks away. We believe in the power of local support and the beauty of a shop that truly knows its people.
