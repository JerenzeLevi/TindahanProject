### 💙 **The Heart of the Project**
This project is deeply personal. I built this for my grandparents, **Guillermo & Lucina**, to honor their hard work and bring a "high-tech" upgrade to their beloved Sari-Sari store. As their eldest grandchild, I wanted to show them how far I've come in my journey and use my skills to make their lives easier.

### 🏠 **The Problem & The Solution**
In our neighborhood, customers often walk all the way to the store only to find out a specific item is out of stock. 
- **The Problem:** Wasted trips and tired legs.
- **The Solution:** This digital storefront serves as a **Live Window**. Neighbors can check the site from their homes to see exactly what is on the shelves before they even start walking. 
- **The Workflow:** While my Aunt manually manages the inventory through the secure admin portal, the community gets a real-time view of available goods.

---

### 🌊 **The Aesthetic**
A tribute to the **Frutiger Aero era (2004-2013)**. It combines the grassroots culture of a Filipino store with the glossy textures, water bubbles, and vibrant optimism of early 2000s UI design. 

### 🚀 **Key Features**
- **💎 Glassmorphic Storefront:** Dynamic product grid with real-time category filtering.
- **🛡️ Secure Admin Portal:** Password-protected dashboard with PHP-encrypted sessions.
- **⚡ Live Inventory Management:** - **Add:** High-gloss upload form with category tagging.
    - **Edit:** Quick-update Qty and Price directly on the card (No refresh needed!).
    - **Status:** Visual badges for "In Stock", "Low Stock", and "Out of Stock".
- **📱 Responsive Aero Design:** A "bubble" UI that fits perfectly on smartphones and tablets.

### 🛠️ **Installation & Setup**
1. **Clone:** `git clone [https://github.com//yourrepo.git](https://github.com/JerenzeLevi/TindahanProject.git)`
2. **Setup:** Update `db.php` with your database credentials. 
3. **Initialize:** Run `admin.php` in your browser—the script will automatically build your tables.
